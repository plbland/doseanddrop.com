Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set up my first medication?

To add a medication:

  1. Tap the "+" button on the home screen
  2. Enter your medication name
  3. Set your dosage calculation method (weight-based or fixed)
  4. Configure your reminder schedule
  5. Save your medication

How does Apple Health integration work?

Dose & Drop can sync with Apple Health to:

  • Automatically read your weight data for dose calculations
  • Write medication records to your Health app
  • Keep your health data in sync across apps

To enable: Go to Settings ‚Üí Health Integration ‚Üí Grant Permissions

Why didn't I receive my medication reminder?

If you're not receiving notifications:

  • Check iOS Settings ‚Üí Notifications ‚Üí Dose & Drop is enabled
  • Ensure Do Not Disturb isn't blocking notifications
  • Verify your medication schedule in the app
  • Make sure the app has permission to send notifications

How do I sync my data across devices?

Dose & Drop uses iCloud to sync your data:

  • Make sure you're signed into iCloud on all devices
  • Enable iCloud sync in app Settings
  • Ensure you have an internet connection
  • Data syncs automatically in the background

Can I use the app offline?

Yes! Dose & Drop works completely offline. All your data is stored locally on your device. You only need an internet connection for iCloud sync.

How accurate are the dose calculations?

Dose & Drop calculates doses based on formulas you configure. However:

  • Always verify calculations with your healthcare provider
  • The app is a tracking tool, not medical advice
  • Double-check all doses before administration
  • Consult your doctor if you have any concerns

How do I delete my data?

To delete your data:

  • Individual medications: Swipe left on any medication and tap Delete
  • All data: Go to Settings ‚Üí Delete All Data
  • Complete removal: Uninstall the app from your device

Note: Deletion cannot be undone unless you have an iCloud backup.

Is my health data private?

Absolutely! Your privacy is our top priority:

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • iCloud sync uses end-to-end encryption
  • We never access or store your health data on our servers
  • See our Privacy Policy for details

Troubleshooting

App Crashes or Freezes

  • Force quit the app and reopen it
  • Restart your device
  • Check for app updates in the App Store
  • Ensure you have the latest iOS version
  • Contact support if the problem persists

Sync Issues

  • Check your internet connection
  • Verify you're signed into iCloud
  • Go to Settings ‚Üí iCloud ‚Üí check Dose & Drop is enabled
  • Try signing out and back into iCloud
  • Allow a few minutes for sync to complete

Incorrect Dose Calculations

  • Verify your weight is up-to-date in Apple Health
  • Check your medication's dosage formula
  • Ensure you've selected the correct calculation method
  • Recalculate by tapping the refresh button
  • Contact your healthcare provider to verify the formula

Account & Subscription Support

Managing Subscriptions

To manage your subscription:

  1. Open iOS Settings
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Select Dose & Drop
  5. Manage or cancel your subscription

Note: Subscription management is handled through Apple, not within the app.

Refund Requests

For refund requests:

  • Visit reportaproblem.apple.com
  • Sign in with your Apple ID
  • Find your Dose & Drop purchase
  • Select "Request a Refund"
