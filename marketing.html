Marketing Information

How We Communicate With You

This page explains how Dose & Drop may communicate with you about our services, updates, and features.

1. Types of Communications

Essential App Notifications

  • Medication Reminders: Timely alerts to help you take your medications
  • Health Data Sync Alerts: Notifications about Apple Health integration
  • Important Updates: Critical app updates and security information

Promotional Communications

  • New Features: Announcements about new app capabilities
  • Tips & Tricks: Helpful information to get the most from the app
  • Special Offers: Information about premium features or promotions

2. Communication Channels

In-App Notifications

Most of our communications happen directly within the app to ensure privacy and convenience:

  • Push notifications for medication reminders
  • In-app banners for feature announcements
  • Alert dialogs for important updates

Email Communications

If you provide your email address, we may send:

  • Account and subscription information
  • Major feature releases and updates
  • Customer support responses
  • Important service announcements

3. Your Communication Preferences

You have full control over how we communicate with you:

Managing Push Notifications

  • Control all notifications through iOS Settings ‚Üí Dose & Drop ‚Üí Notifications
  • Customize medication reminder schedules within the app
  • Choose which types of alerts you want to receive

Managing Email Communications

  • Unsubscribe from promotional emails using the link in any email
  • Update your email preferences in the app settings
  • Note: You cannot unsubscribe from essential service emails

4. What We Don't Do

To protect your privacy and ensure a quality experience:

  • No Third-Party Sharing: We never sell or share your contact information
  • No Spam: We limit marketing communications to relevant, helpful information
  • No Unsolicited Calls: We will never call you for marketing purposes
  • No SMS Marketing: We don't send promotional text messages

5. Testimonials and User Feedback

We may feature user testimonials and feedback in our marketing materials:

  • We only use feedback with explicit user permission
  • Personal health information is never shared
  • You can request removal of your testimonial at any time

6. Social Media

Follow us on social media for:

  • Product updates and announcements
  • Health and wellness tips
  • Community discussions
  • Customer support

7. App Store Communications

We encourage users to:

  • Leave honest reviews on the App Store
  • Share feedback about features you'd like to see
  • Report any issues you encounter

8. Frequency of Communications

Medication Reminders: As scheduled by you (multiple times daily)

Feature Updates: 1-2 times per month (when new features are released)

Promotional Emails: Maximum 1-2 per month

Service Announcements: Only when necessary

9. Contact Preferences

If you have questions about our marketing communications or want to update your preferences, please contact us at:

Email: support@dosedrop.app

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update our marketing communications policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through:

  • In-app notifications
  • Email announcements
  • Updates to this page
