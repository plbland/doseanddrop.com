Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 4, 2025

Your Privacy Matters

Dose & Drop is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection

Dose & Drop stores all your health data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal health information on our servers.

  • Weight measurements
  • Medication tracking data
  • Nutrition logs
  • Personal settings and preferences

Apple Health Integration

If you choose to enable Apple Health integration, Dose & Drop will:

  • Read weight data from Apple Health (with your permission)
  • Write weight data to Apple Health (with your permission)
  • All data remains within Apple's ecosystem and your device

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using secure iOS storage mechanisms. Your data is never transmitted to external servers or third parties.

Analytics and Tracking

Dose & Drop does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising services. We do not collect usage statistics or behavioral data.

Third-Party Services

Dose & Drop does not integrate with any third-party services except for Apple Health (which is optional and requires your explicit permission).

Data Security

Your data is protected by:

  • iOS device encryption
  • Secure local storage
  • No cloud synchronization (unless you use iCloud backup for your entire device)
  • No account creation required

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Delete any or all data at any time
  • No need to request data deletion from us
  • Your data never leaves your device

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@dosedrop.app
