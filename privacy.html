Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 15, 2025

At Dose & Drop, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our iOS application.

1. Information We Collect

Health Data

  • Medication schedules and dosage information
  • Weight measurements synced with Apple Health
  • Medication adherence tracking
  • User-entered health notes and observations

Apple Health Integration

With your explicit permission, Dose & Drop integrates with Apple Health to:

  • Read weight data to automatically update your medication dosages
  • Write medication data for comprehensive health tracking
  • Sync your medication schedule with Health app

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide accurate medication dose calculations based on your weight
  • Send you timely medication reminders
  • Sync your health data across your devices via iCloud
  • Improve app functionality and user experience

3. Data Storage and Security

Your privacy is paramount:

  • Local Storage: All your health data is stored locally on your device
  • iCloud Sync: Data is synced via your private iCloud account with end-to-end encryption
  • No Server Storage: We do not store your health information on our servers
  • Apple Health: Health data is protected by iOS security features and Apple's privacy policies

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal health information with third parties. Your data remains private and is only accessible to you through:

  • The Dose & Drop app on your devices
  • Apple Health (only with your permission)
  • Your iCloud account (for sync purposes)

5. Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all your data within the app
  • Modification: Edit or update your information at any time
  • Deletion: Delete your data by removing the app from your device
  • Apple Health Permissions: Revoke access at any time through iOS Settings

6. Children's Privacy

Dose & Drop is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Sending an in-app notification for significant changes

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at:

Email: support@dosedrop.app

9. Apple Health Disclaimer

Dose & Drop is not a medical device and should not be used for medical diagnosis or treatment. Always consult with your healthcare provider for medical advice. The app is designed to help you track and manage your medications, but you are responsible for verifying all dosage calculations with your healthcare provider.
